Quoted: The First Post

Jan 24th, 2015

I downloaded Quotebook when it first came out, then sort of forgot about it. Recently, however, I’ve been using it more and more especially since I started reading again.

I decided to begin sharing some of my favorite quotes on this site every now and then. Here’s three.

“Too often, we grab the hand and—without realizing it—walk off without the suitcase.” — Ed Catmull in Creativity, Inc.

 

“When you’re in your little room and you’re working on something good but if it’s really good you’re gonna need a bigger room and when you’re in the bigger room you might not know what to do you might have to think of how you got started sitting in your litle room.” — The White Stripes in Little Room

 

“People like to listen to other people talking. That’s the business plan.” — Myke Hurley

That last one’s just great.
